Grades zilizotumika katika Calculator hii:

  • A: = 70 – 100     Excellent
  • B+: = 65 – 69     Very Good
  • B: = 60 – 64     Good
  • C: = 50 – 59     Satisfactory
  • D: = 40 – 49     Marginal Fail
  • E: = 0 – 39     Absolute Fail

Final GPA imekokotolewa kupitia mahesabu haya:
Grade Hesabu GPA
A Y = 0.02X + 3 4.4 – 5.0
B+ Y = 0.08X – 1.2 4.0 – 4.3
B Y = 0.2X – 9 3.0 – 3.9
C Y = 0.1X – 3 2.0 – 2.9
D Y = 0.1X - 3 1.0 – 1.9
E Y = (0.025)X 0 – 0.9
